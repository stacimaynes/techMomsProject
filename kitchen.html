KITCHEN MAINTENANCE
    1. Clean refrigerator coils (pull out, unplug and vacuum) once a year.
    2. Seal Countertops 2 times a year.
    3. Clean the furnace filter to remove dust build-ups, make it easier to regulate your home's temperature, and ultimately decrease energy bills.
    4. Clean the garbage disposal by grinding ice cubes, then flushing with hot water and baking soda.
    5. Clean kitchen exhaust fan filter.
    6. Seal tile grout.
    7. Clean under the refrigerator.
    8. Clean the oven.
    9. Clean the dishwasher drain.
    10. Clean Out Exhaust Fan
