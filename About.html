ABOUT US

We have lived in our home for 10 years and everything for the first 8-9 years was great until we realized lack of maintenance around our house was causing us bigger problems. Things that we didn't realize and no one told us at the time as first time home builders and because we had a new build it took longer for us to realize. We don't want anyone else to have this problem in their homes and we realized how easy it is to keep things nice, clean, budget friendly and manageable if everything was given to you all in one place and things that no one tells you as a first time home owner.. or maybe even a long time home owner... well... we are going to tell you!
